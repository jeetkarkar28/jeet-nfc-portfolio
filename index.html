About Me

I am an Automation Engineer at Hi-Con Technocast Pvt. Ltd., having transitioned from my initial role in Production Planning & Control (PPC). Combining classical mechanical principles with Industry 4.0 tools, I design smart, data-driven systems that streamline assembly floor monitoring, reduce paper logs, and optimize predictive maintenance workflows.

My core competencies include engineering industrial IoT dashboards for real-time telemetry, deploying RFID-based tracking systems for identification, and automating manual shop floor reporting using Excel VBA. I focus on converting raw manufacturing logs into visual dashboards that enable plant supervisors to make fast, accurate operational decisions.

Integrate digital agility with industrial resilience to build the factories of tomorrow.
